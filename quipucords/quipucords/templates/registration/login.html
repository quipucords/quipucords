Welcome to Red Hat Entitlements Reporting! Entitlements Reporting is an inspection and reporting tool to identify environment data, or facts, such as the number of physical and virtual systems on a network, their operating systems and other configuration data, and versions of some key packages and products for almost any Linux or UNIX version. The ability to inspect the software and systems that are running on your network improves your ability to understand and report on your entitlement usage. Ultimately, this inspection and reporting process is part of the larger system administration task of managing your inventories.
